Wires and Cables!

Electric cables are the often-overlooked champions of modern life, silently supplying power to our residences, businesses, and industrial operations. Despite their unpretentious appearance, these wires play a vital role in facilitating the smooth flow of electricity, ensuring the seamless functioning of our devices and the efficient operation of our appliances.

Regarding grounding and electrical installation cables, our offerings span a broad spectrum of armored cables. This range encompasses single-core armored cables, as well as multi-core XLPE/PVC/AWA armored cables, with a voltage rating of 600-1000V, providing protection for specific applications such as traffic signals and more. As a prominent supplier of XLPE cables, we also provide a complementary assortment of high-quality cable accessories, meticulously designed and tested to ensure a precise fit with our cables.

Applications!

These cables are commonly employed for providing main electricity supply and serving as backup control cables in various settings, such as outdoors, indoors, experimental systems, and aerial power networks in cable ducting, among others. As a reliable electrical cable supplier based in Dubai, we specialize in offering low-smoke, flame-resistant rubber cables that excel in wiring solutions for commercial buildings, residential complexes, hotels, educational institutes, hospitals, and more. These cables not only ensure superior safety during fire incidents by minimizing smoke and toxic gas emissions but also effectively contain the spread of fire. With their weather and fire resistance, alongside high mechanical strength, they prove to be ideal for industrial usage, renewable energy projects, telecommunications, data centers, transportation systems, military applications, emergency services, infrastructure projects, and customized solutions, all while adhering to global safety standards and certifications.

Features!
  • Boasting prolonged durability and fire resistance, it remains stable without melting or dripping.
  • Proving highly effective during fire rescue operations, the insulation material exhibits slow combustion.
  • The conductor facilitates optimal connectivity at terminals and sockets.
  • They are assigned specific voltage levels to ensure safe operation.
  • They are suitable for flexing and movement in versatile applications.
