Drainage Accessories !

Roof drainage systems play a crucial role, especially in regions with extended periods of wet and rainy weather. Maintaining these drains is vital to ensure their efficient operation and protect against the elements. The last thing anyone wants is to face the hurricane season with clogged roof drains. Primarily, these drains are designed to divert excess water, typically caused by rainfall. Equipped with a cast iron grate, they prevent debris from obstructing the water flow down the drainage system. The primary function of these drains is to prevent water from accumulating on the roof's surface, safeguarding homes from water damage.

Strategically positioned at the roof's lowest points, these drainage systems are commonly installed in semi-flat or flat roofs, where natural runoff isn't as readily available as in conventional pitched roofs. Roof drains come in various materials such as synthetic, metal, or cast iron, each varying in cost and durability. They play a vital role in protecting structures, their occupants, and the contents beneath, shielding homes from the elements like sun, wind, and rain. A sound roof drainage system serves as a vital component in protecting homes by effectively controlling water flow and preventing potential damage.

Additionally, cast iron roof drains are used to minimize the accumulation of groundwater around the house's foundation. Water pooling on the roof can directly impact the structural integrity of the building, leading to potential cave-ins or leaks. Efficient roof drainage systems can prevent both long and short-term damage, making the roof an economical and low-maintenance investment.

We offer 8” diameter roof drains, featuring a coated cast iron body with a combination membrane flashing clamp/gravel guard and an aluminum dome. Our range includes various pipe sizes and outlet types to meet your specific requirements.

Floor Drains!

Floor Drains are commonly employed in spaces such as kitchens, washrooms, laundries, refrigerator areas, locker rooms, and swimming pool areas. They come in various sizes and designs, tailored to fit different applications.

TradeX Greens provides an extensive array of Drains, including S.S Square drains, S.S Linear Drain, S.S Recessed Drain, S.S Solid Top Drains, Stainless Steel Tile Drains, and various Custom Made Drains manufactured to order

The types available are as follows:

Stainless Steel Square Drains:

These are commonly utilized in showers, typically positioned at the center of the shower floor. The floor is inclined from all directions towards the drain, acting as a gentle slope funneling all water into the drain.

Stainless Steel Linear Drains::

Linear Drains are specifically designed with a linear pattern, often placed either in the center of the floor or along one wall of the shower. These drains ensure the entire shower floor slopes uniformly and smoothly toward the drain, eliminating the need for a funnel. Linear drains come in various lengths, offering diverse installation possibilities.
