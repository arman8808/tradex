Single Products Solutions

SS Manhole Covers!

Offered in galvanized steel, and stainless steel, these covers are crafted for a diverse range of applications. They come in both recessed and solid top models, with the choice of single or double seals.

These covers are available in drop-in, hinged, or lockable variations. They are reinforced to ensure added strength and durability. For larger openings, it is advisable to use multiple covers with removable cross supports, specifically designed to manage heavier loads. These covers are removable for easy maintenance.

Hinged covers are furnished with robust hinges and locks to guarantee longevity and effortless operation. Additionally, covers equipped with a spring-assisted lifting mechanism can be provided upon request.

300 Series Manhole Cover!

The 300 Series Stainless Steel Recessed Manhole Covers are suitable for utilization with various concrete and paving block types. Equipped with locking mechanisms and neoprene gasket seals all around, these covers are ideal for applications in shopping malls, hospitals, and similar environments.

Recessed Covers with Thin Rim!

For light-duty applications, our galvanized steel Thin Rim Recessed Manhole Covers are perfect for tiled paving areas where maintaining surface finish and pattern continuity is crucial. These recessed manhole covers are suitable for both internal and external use.

Hinged Water Tank Covers!

The MS Galvanized Hinged Covers feature raised non-slip solid top with a chequered pattern. Ample reinforcing stiffeners are included to withstand cover flexing under varying load conditions. These hinged covers serve as both floor access covers and water tank covers. Equipped with locking mechanisms and self-locking safety bars, the hinged covers securely maintain their position.

Manhole Covers in Multiple Sections!

Multi-part covers with detachable cross beams, featuring solid top drop-in types, are designed with a raised non-slip chequered pattern. Sufficient reinforcing stiffeners are incorporated to counteract cover flexing during varying load conditions. Additionally, covers can be customized with a peep-in feature for situations that require frequent access.

Multi-Part Covers in Triangular Sections!

These Solid Top Drop-In Type Covers are furnished with raised, non-slip chequered patterns. Sufficient reinforcing stiffeners are integrated to counteract cover flexing during load conditions.

Recessed Manhole Covers for Paving Blocks!

The MS Galvanized Recessed Pavior Type Multipart Covers are compatible with all varieties of concrete and paving blocks up to a depth of 100mm. Sufficient reinforcing stiffeners are included to prevent flexing under varying loads.

Multi-Section Covers with Detachable Beams!

The MS Galvanized Recessed Pavior Type Multi-Part Covers, featuring removable beams, are compatible with all types of concrete and paving blocks up to 100mm in depth. Sufficient reinforcing stiffeners are integrated to withstand flexing under load.
